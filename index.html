F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it a consultancy?

    No, We are Technology Company and we offer our client project specific training and placement.

  • Already trained and certified then why should we take up the training and pay the fees?

    We work on a program called HTD (Hire Train & Deploy). Training is completely only on job description

  • Can we pay the fees in instalments?

    No, we don’t have that option.

  • Is there any concession/Discount in fee?

    No.

  • Without training is it possible to get the job?

    No. How many interviews did u attend and did you clear any?

  • Is it an on roll or payroll job?

    It depends on the company and their clients you get selected for.

  • If we join as payroll are there any chances of getting on roll?

    It depends on your work performance.

  • We are already working and 3+ experience we want only placement?

    We do consider if the profile matches our company requirement.

  • What are you training?

    Training is given based on Job description, Office Communication, profile presentation, Mock Interviews, Corporate etiquettes

  • Is the employment contract or permanent?

    Permanent

  • For how long do you provide job guarantee?

    We give Job technical support for a year

  • Will they fire after joining?

    No, Why do you they will fire? When your behavior and performance is good

  • Are we paid during training period?

    No

  • What about the accommodation during the training process? Does it include with the training fees?

    No

  • We want higher salary package with lesser fees?

    For each level we have fixed fees based on the Training contents and depth.

  • What are the facilities given from the company?

    It varies from Client.

  • Can you deduct fees from our salary even after submitting original documents?

    No.

  • Can we interact with already placed candidates?

    Yes.

  • Why there is difference between CTC and take-home salary?

    In CTC, PF and ESI benefits are included and it is deducted in the take home salary.
